About Us

Pixelency is a creative digital studio that designs award-winning websites and brand experiences. We’re driven by a passion for clean, functional design — and a belief that great design should be both beautiful and strategic.
Founded in France, we’ve grown into one of the most recognized creative teams in the country, earning global recognition for our work and our innovation in digital design.
We’re now opening our doors to aspiring designers who want to learn, grow, and create real impact through design.

About the Position

We’re looking for a UI/UX Design Apprentice to support our design team in crafting user-first, modern web interfaces. This is a hands-on apprenticeship — you’ll work closely with senior designers and developers to build digital experiences across various industries.
You should have a solid understanding of design fundamentals and a willingness to learn, iterate, and collaborate in a fast-paced, creative environment.

Responsibilities

  • Design user-centric interfaces — Support in creating clear, functional, and visually Compelling layouts across devices.

  • Contribute to projects — Assist in wireframing, prototyping, and delivering UI assets for web and mobile projects.

  • Follow design systems — Learn and apply existing guidelines while contributing to evolving design standards.

  • Collaborate with the team — Work closely with developers, clients, and other designers to bring ideas to life.

  • Learn by doing — Get feedback, iterate quickly, and explore new tools and practices in real-world projects.

Profile
Hard Skills:

  • Basic experience with web or product design (personal or school projects welcome!)

  • Knowledge of Figma (auto layout, components, variables).

  • Understanding of design principles (layout, spacing, visual hierarchy).

  • Curious about user behavior and usability.

  • Comfortable with feedback and iteration.

Soft Skills:

  • Strong motivation to learn and grow.

  • Good communicator and team player.

  • Attention to detail and love for clean, structured design.

  • Organized, self-driven, and responsible with time and tasks.

Work Environment

  • Creative culture — We value ideas, collaboration, and initiative at every level.

  • Real projects — Work on actual client briefs with guidance from experienced designers.

  • Supportive Mentorship — Get feedback, advice, and coaching to develop your design mindset.

  • Flexible & Remote — Work from anywhere in France, with optional in-person meetups.

  • Growth-Focused — Learn fast, build your portfolio, and become job-ready by the end of the program.

Benefits

  • Paid apprenticeship (depending on contract terms)

  • 100% remote setup

  • Flexible working hours

  • Access to professional design tools (Figma, Notion, etc.)

  • Learning budget for courses, books, or workshops

  • Personalized mentoring from senior designers
